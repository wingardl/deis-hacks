About

Waltham Partnership For Youth

WPY’s transportation study’s main goal is to help those in transit, this site will allow students to give each other tips and ideas on how to get around, problems that are up in certain areas as well as slow buses and bus changes – things that also go into the site are issues surrounding certain neighborhoods, from side walks that need to be repaved or even places where side walks are needed. The information that we learn from the study can eventually also go into the site to help promote and inform people of everyday life in Waltham. And while yes some beautification issues surrounding Waltham cannot be completed with a simple click of a button, the more people who are aware of the problems, and want to help can help by signing petitions, raising money, going to government officials to ensure Waltham stays a safe and accessible for everyone. In many ways this is similar to a 311 for students and parents.
