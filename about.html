About Us



Waltham Partnership For Youth's Waltham Transport Hub

Transport Hub's primary goal is to help youth navigate their transit. Given that many youth do not drive, it is necessary that alternate transportation routes (walking, biking, public transit, etc) are safe and accessible.

Whether a student sees a particular block that could be made safer, finds themself waiting for a heavily delayed bus, or enjoys a particular walk along the Charles, this website can be of help. On top of individual safety, this site thrives on crowdsourcing! Students can give each other first-hand tips for getting around -- both positive and negative.

This tool will also help WPY learn. We are excited to engage democratically with the youth of Waltham and listen to their first hand experiences and knowledge, and shift our programs and resources toward their actual needs.

Ideally, this site can be a jumping-off point for political and community engagement where all residents -- young or old -- can work toward action. Signing petitions, raising money, or going to government officials can ensure Waltham stays safe and accessible for everyone, and we'd like to help in any way we can. Our community deserves a way to enrich and improve our infrastructure without the barriers of entry or harm that are an unfortunate reality when interacting with government or law enforcement. We hope to provide a helpful service to our peers, friends, and neighbors, so please let us know if you think of something we can improve!

Thank you!
